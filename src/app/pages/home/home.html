ABOUT SHRUTI INDUSTRIES

Shruti Industries is a dynamic and diversified enterprise, actively operating across automotive, information technology, and transportation sectors with multiple cutting-edge projects under its belt.

In the automotive sector, we are proud manufacturers of precision-engineered components for two and three-wheelers, including Engine Brackets, PVC Clamps, Magnet Puller Plates, Z Brackets, Cable Clips, and more — all designed to meet the evolving demands of leading OEMs and aftermarket clients.

Our IT division is at the forefront of innovation, building scalable digital platforms such as LMS (Learning Management System) for education, HLT (Hot Lead Tracker) for sales pipeline management, and Examo, a robust online examination system tailored for institutions and training providers.

In the transportation domain, we operate under the brand Sanjivani, managing a fleet of 22 commercial vehicles that ensure seamless logistics and cargo movement across India, reinforcing our reputation for reliability and nationwide reach.

With a firm commitment to quality, technology, and customer satisfaction, Shruti Industries continues to shape the future across every industry we serve.
